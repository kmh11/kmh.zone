Simple

Storing a password is as simple as typing a password and a username. No fussing with master passwords or worrying about forgetting your main password. It's as simple as type, click, done!

Secure

No passwords are stored in plain text. They are all stored as ones and zeros, in what is known as "binary" making it nearly impossible for anything but a machine to view passwords (unless you are using our secure interface).

Publicly Verifiable

No more wondering whether somebody is lying about their identity. You can simply verify their password with the one in our database, giving you peace of mind that you are talking to who you think you are.
